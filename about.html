A movement led by refugee youth, for refugee youth.

Our Mission

To unite refugee youth in Malaysia in a safe space where we find strength in each other, empower ourselves through education, skills and advocacy, and turn our hopes into reality — reclaiming our voices and the confidence to shape our own futures.

Our Vision

A future where refugee youth are not just seen but heard, not just surviving but thriving — with the strength to stand tall, the empowerment to break barriers, and the hope to build a life of dignity and purpose.

Meet Our Founder

Areej Khan

Founder & CEO · Refugee Youth Advocate

Areej arrived in Malaysia as a refugee at the age of twelve. The challenges she grew up with became the question that drives her: what can be done to make things better for refugee youth? That question became SEHER.

A refugee youth advocate and programme designer, Areej has turned lived experience into leadership — and into action. She founded SEHER to build the belonging, support, and opportunity she wishes she'd had growing up.

  • Spoke at Malaysia's Parliament twice to advocate for refugee rights
  • Designed & led advocacy camps that build awareness and amplify youth voices
  • Trained through ASEAN-region advocacy & leadership programmes

Today, SEHER is led by Areej alone — but this is only the dawn. A team of refugee youth leaders will grow around it.
